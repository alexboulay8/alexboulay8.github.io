Parameter

noun

pa-ram-e-ter

    1. an arbitrary constant whose value characterizes a member of a system (such as a family of curves)
      also: a quantity (such as a mean or variance) that describes a statistical population
    2. an independent variable used to express coordinates of a variable point and functions of them
    1. any of a set of physical properties whose values determine the characteristics or behavior of something
    1. something represented by a parameter: a characteristic element
